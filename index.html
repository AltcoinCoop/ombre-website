ABOUT

The Ombre Project is dedicated to making a fast, private, equitable and accessible cryptocurrency.

  • An Ombre transaction is expected to take only 20 minutes.
  • Ombre is truly fungible, untraceable and decentrilized.
  • There was no premine. A novel funding system using a dynamic developer fee was created to ensure equitable distribution of coins.
  • The Ombre Project is actively researching ways to make the currency easier to use by people with minimal technical experience. We believe Ombre is the cash of the future.
