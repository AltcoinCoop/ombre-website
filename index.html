Introducing Ombre.

Secure. Private. Untraceable. Actively developed.

Our mission is to enhance the CryptoNote protocol by providing a lightweight, well maintained, intuitive, no-nonsense coin.

  • A 60 second block time increases transaction speed, without comprimising security.
  • An enforced ring-mixin size of 10 ensures safety from blockchain analysis.
  • A transparent developer funding system built into the coin ensures long-term active development without requiring trust from the community.
  • Other CryptoNote coins are treated as collaborators rather than competitors.
